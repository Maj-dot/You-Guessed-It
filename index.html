Welcome to Just Guess It!

Objective: Our space-woman "Nola" is trapped on the planet Earth! Let's help her get to her sneakership so she can phone home!

Guess the correct sneaker brand before running out of attempts.

    HOW TO PLAY:
  • Click on a letter from the onscreen keyboard to make a guess.
  • If your guess is in the word, it will appear in the display and Nola will start to appear!
  • If your guess is not in the word, it will count as an incorrect guess.
  • If you make 6 incorrect guesses, the game is over!

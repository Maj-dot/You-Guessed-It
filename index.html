Lets Guess The Sneaker Brand!

Objective: Our spacewoman is trapped on the planet Earth! Let's help her get to her sneakership so she can phone home!

Guess the correct word before running out of attempts.

    HOW TO PLAY
  • Click on a letter from the keyboard to make a guess.
  • If your guess is in the word, it will appear in the display and our spacewoman will start to appear!
  • If your guess is not in the word, it will count as an incorrect guess.
  • If you make 6 incorrect guesses, the game is over!
